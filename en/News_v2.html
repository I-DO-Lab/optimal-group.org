Congratulations to Prof. Yuanhai Shao for Being Ranked in Top 0.05% Scientists Globally
Recently, ScholarGPS released the 2024 list of the world's top 0.05% scientists. Prof. Yuanhai Shao, the group leader, was ranked 14th in the "Lifetime" list for Support Vector Machine research. ScholarGPS collects information on over 30 million scholars worldwide and ranks them based on research output, impact, and paper quality. In 2024, over 200 scholars from mainland China were selected for the "Lifetime" list. Prof. Shao's selection demonstrates the global influence of OPTIMAL Group in this research field.

Prof. Yuanhai Shao Appointed as Editorial Board Member of Neural Networks
Recently, Prof. Yuanhai Shao was invited to serve as an Editorial Board Member of Neural Networks, a prestigious journal published by Elsevier. Founded in 1988, Neural Networks is a top-tier SCI Q1 journal in engineering, computer science, and artificial intelligence with an impact factor of 7.8 (2023). The journal covers computer science, mathematics, engineering, psychology, AI, and neuroscience, and has high academic reputation in the field of neural networks and artificial intelligence.

Prof. Chunna Li Published Paper in Machine Learning Journal
The paper "Group feature selection using non-class data" by Prof. Chunna Li, Prof. Weijie Chen, Prof. Yuanhai Shao (corresponding author), Yuangang Pan from A*STAR, and Prof. Ivor W. Tsang from University of Technology Sydney has been published in Machine Learning (IF=2.9). Machine Learning, published by Springer since 1986, is a CCF-recommended Class B journal in artificial intelligence. The paper discovers that F∞-norm SVM implicitly uses non-class data during group feature selection and improves the algorithm to fully exploit the classification information provided by these data.
