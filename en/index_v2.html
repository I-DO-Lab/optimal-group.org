About Us

OPTIMAL Group is a research group organized by Dr. Yuan-Hai Shao. "OPTIMAL" means optimization for intellegent machine learning. The group has been formed since 2011 to study the theory, models, algorithms, and applications of Optimization for intellegent machines.
